05.How to turn white into any color.

  • Open Your Image in Photoshop: By now if you followed the other five Tutorials it should be easy if not, just drag the image that you want in phtoshop to open iy.
  • Select the T-Shirt: or what you want to change the color i my case it's the mans t-shirt.
  • - Use the Quick Selection Tool from the toolbar on the left (or press `W` on your keyboard).
  • - Click and drag the Quick Selection Tool over the white t-shirt to select it.
  • - If any unwanted areas are selected, hold down the `Alt` key (Windows) or `Option` key (Mac) and click on those areas to deselect them.
  • Create a Solid Color Adjustment Layer:
  • - With the t-shirt selected, go to `Layer > New Fill Layer > Solid Color`.
  • - In the dialog box that appears, you can name the layer if you wish, then click `OK`.
  • - Choose the color you want for the t-shirt from the Color Picker and click `OK`.
  • Change the Blending Mode:
  • - In the Layers panel, make sure the new Solid Color fill layer is selected.
  • - Change the blending mode of this layer to `Linear Burn` from the dropdown menu at the top of the Layers panel.
  • Feather the Edges for a More Realistic Effect:
  • - Double-click on the Solid Color fill layer thumbnail to open the Layer Style dialog box.
  • - In the dialog box, go to the "Blending Options" section.
  • - Adjust the `Feather` slider under the "Blend If" section. This will soften the edges of the color change, making it look more realistic. Click `OK` when done.
  • Refine the Mask:
  • - If necessary, refine the mask by painting on the mask thumbnail in the Layers panel.
  • - Select the mask thumbnail of the Solid Color fill layer.
  • - Use a soft brush to paint with black to hide or white to reveal areas of the color adjustment, refining the edges and making sure the color change looks natural.
  • Save Your Image:
  • - Save your edited image by going to `File > Save As` and choose the desired format and location.
    • That's it! You've successfully changed the color of the white t-shirt to any color you chose and added a feather effect for a more realistic appearance.
