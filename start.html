About this Project

The Targeted Group

The purpose of my project is to create an instruction page for Photoshop, aimed at different people interested in learning how to use the programme. By offering clear and easy-to-understand instructions, I want to make it easier for beginners to get started with Photoshop and explore its features. The goal of the project is to create six project steps to cover different basic functions and techniques in Photoshop. These steps will provide a basic understanding of the programme and give users the confidence to explore more advanced features on their own. The target audience for my instruction page is varied and includes people with different backgrounds and levels of experience in Photoshop. From total beginners who have never used the programme before to more experienced users who want to improve their skills and learn new techniques. By adapting the instructions to a wide audience, I aim to make them accessible and useful to anyone interested in learning Photoshop.
